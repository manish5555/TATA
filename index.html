WELCOME TO TATA STEEL UK LEGACY TEAM


Team which transforms business requirements into reality of Steel Manufacturing. Dive into history of TATA STEEL EUROPE.

The team comprises of 40 experienced and enthusiastic associates working in different applications. The Applications are OASIS,STACA,COMPASS,TROSTRE and EMH. The team was formed in the year 2012 when TCS was selected as the technological partner of TATA STEEL UK . The first project that the team handled was Supply Chain Transformation implementation in Tata Steel UK Sphere.
